## Findings

### [BLOCKING] Home.md header newline: spec vs. test conflict
**Location:** Batch 4 / Card 9 vs. Card 10 test (a)
**Issue:** Card 9 defines the header as `"# Tasks\n\n"` (double newline); Card 10 test (a) asserts empty-list output is `"# Tasks\n"` (single newline). Both cannot be correct — one path fails at verify.
**Fix:** Align both to `"# Tasks\n\n"` (matching non-empty rendering where a blank line precedes the first bucket) and update test (a) to expect `"# Tasks\n\n"`.

### [BLOCKING] WIKI_SKIP_PUSH suppresses pull, contradicting shared decision
**Location:** Batch 6 / Card 15 `writeOp` step (2) vs. `## Shared Decisions → env vars for test isolation`
**Issue:** Card 15 gates `pull` on `WIKI_SKIP_GIT != "1" AND WIKI_SKIP_PUSH != "1"`, so `WIKI_SKIP_PUSH=1` skips the pull. The shared decision explicitly states `WIKI_SKIP_PUSH=1` means "pull + commit, skip push" — pull must still run.
**Fix:** Change step (2) condition to `WIKI_SKIP_GIT != "1"` only; let `commitPush` (which already checks `WIKI_SKIP_PUSH`) handle the push gate.

### [NIT] BriefTask struct location is ambiguous
**Location:** Batch 2 / Card 5
**Issue:** Requirements say "Define `BriefTask` struct (in `store.go` or `task.go`)" — leaving the choice open risks diverging implementations across agents.
**Fix:** Pin it to `store.go` (it is only consumed by store methods and wiki.go).

### [NIT] Card 11 Context over-includes task.go
**Location:** Batch 5 / Card 11
**Issue:** `git.go` references no types from `task.go`; the Context entry is noise.
**Fix:** Remove `internal/wiki/task.go` from Card 11 Context.
