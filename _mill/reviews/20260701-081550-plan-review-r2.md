### [BLOCKING] dispatch signature change breaks integration test
**Location:** Batch 1 / Card 5
**Issue:** Card 5 adds a `setFlags []string` parameter to `dispatch(...)`, but `internal/configcli/configcli_integration_test.go:83` calls `dispatch(hostLayout, os.Stdin, &out, []string{"warp"}, fakeEdit, injectedSync, false)` with the old 7-arg signature; that file (`//go:build integration`) is not in Card 5's `Edits` nor in `All Files Touched`, so it will not compile under `-tags integration`. The batch `verify` runs without the integration tag, so it silently masks the break.
**Fix:** Add `internal/configcli/configcli_integration_test.go` to Card 5's `Edits` and update its `dispatch(...)` call to pass `nil` for the new `setFlags` argument.

### [NIT] boardengine git-stderr leaks left uncovered
**Location:** Batches 2-3 (scope)
**Issue:** A repo-wide scan shows three more raw git-stderr interpolations of the same shape at `internal/boardengine/git.go:27` and `:100` and `internal/boardengine/sync.go:133` (`pull failed: %s`/`push failed: %s`, stderr); the plan's Shared Decision scopes itself to the "config/warp/weft surface" and omits them, leaving the convention only partially applied.
**Fix:** Either add a boardengine card mirroring the warpengine edits, or note explicitly in `00-overview.md` why boardengine is deliberately out of scope for this task.
